Spring Salad

Ingredients

  • Broccoli Microgreens
  • Spinach
  • Fresh Strawberries
  • Green Onion
  • Poppy Seed Dressing

Steps

  • Cut strawberries and green onion to desired size
  • Mix together with spinach
  • Serve with dressing
  • Enjoy!
